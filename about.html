Our Story

SecondChapter Books began in 2010 with a simple mission: to give pre-loved books a new home and make reading accessible to everyone. What started as a small corner shop with a few hundred books has grown into a community hub for book lovers of all ages.

We believe that books, like people, have many lives to live. Each book on our shelves has a history - it has been read, enjoyed, and now waits for its next reader to discover the worlds within its pages.

Our carefully curated collection includes everything from timeless classics to contemporary bestsellers, all at affordable prices that make building your personal library accessible to everyone.

Our Values

Sustainability

By giving books a second life, we reduce waste and help protect the environment. Each book reused means one less book that ends up in a landfill.

Community

We're more than just a bookstore; we're a gathering place for readers, writers, and storytellers to connect and share their passion for literature.

Accessibility

We believe everyone should have access to books. Our affordable pricing makes reading accessible to all, regardless of budget.

Diversity

We curate our collection to represent diverse voices and perspectives, ensuring our readers can find stories that both mirror and expand their worldview.
